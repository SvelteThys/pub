Svelte is a young Belgian artist, known for her small paintings inspired by her hiking trips in nature, close to her home. These hiking trips are special, it’s a route she is making all most her entire life, which brings her back to her childhood. That’s why her paintings represent this frisky feeling. Her drawings are made on such hiking trips, and can be seen as a reminder for what can later become a painting.
